About me

I was born in Tianjin, China and live in Uppsala, Sweden now. Read about some of my interests below, and if you want to find out more about a particular topic maybe I wrote about it in my blog.

Some things that keep me occupied...

I'm working on one project about GSM networks. More informations are in my blog.


There are some projects I made Slackware packages for. Some may be outdated, that's because you can now find them on repositories like slacky or linuxpackages. For software that I had to patch, or build from development snapshots, you will find SlackBuilds or Arch PKGBUILDs.

Awesome is an "awesome" window manager I use. My config is available here. After some years of using FVWM2 and Ion I completely switched to a tiling window manager. I recommend you to read the Ion manifesto as tiling wm's could just be the thing your were searching for. For a while I used FVWM2 on my main workstation, my config mimics QNX Photon but with some modern GNU/Linux desktop stuff like notification daemon, session mngmt., mouse gestures, systray, Clearlooks, Tango...
